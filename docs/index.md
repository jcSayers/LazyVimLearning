# Welcome to the Vim & Neovim Tutorial

This is a **self-contained, comprehensive guide** to learning Vim and Neovim. Every page is designed to teach you through reading, examples, and interactive practice‚Äîno AI assistance required once you start.

## Why Vim?

Vim is a powerful, ubiquitous text editor that has remained relevant for over 30 years. Once you understand its philosophy of **modal editing**, you'll experience:

- **Speed**: Accomplish text edits in fewer keystrokes
- **Efficiency**: Keep your hands on the keyboard without reaching for the mouse
- **Portability**: Vim is available on virtually every system
- **Extensibility**: Configure and customize to your exact workflow

## What You'll Learn

### Fundamentals Section
- **Modes** - Normal, Insert, Visual, Command-line
- **Navigation** - Efficient movement through files
- **Editing** - Inserting, deleting, changing text
- **Search & Replace** - Finding and replacing text patterns
- **Files & Buffers** - Working with multiple files

### Intermediate Section
- **Text Objects** - The cornerstone of Vim's power
- **Advanced Motions** - Moving like a Vim expert
- **Marks & Jumps** - Navigate between locations
- **Macros** - Record and repeat command sequences
- **Registers** - Master the clipboard system
- **Windows & Tabs** - Work with multiple views

### Advanced Section
- **Vimscript Basics** - Automate your workflow
- **Configuration** - Customize .vimrc or init.vim
- **Plugins** - Extend Vim with community tools
- **Neovim Specific** - Modern configuration with Lua
- **Advanced Patterns** - Complex editing scenarios
