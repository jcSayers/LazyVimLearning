## Tips

1. **Read Incrementally** - Don't try to learn everything at once
2. **Practice Daily** - 15 minutes of practice beats 2 hours of reading
3. **Focus on Core** - Master Vim before jumping to plugins
4. **Join Communities** - Learning with others helps
5. **Share Knowledge** - Teaching reinforces learning
